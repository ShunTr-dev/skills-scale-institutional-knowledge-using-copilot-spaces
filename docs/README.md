# OctoAcme Project Management Docs

This folder contains the process documentation for OctoAcme project management. Use it as your starting point to understand how we plan, execute, and continuously improve the delivery of product features, services, and integrations.

## Overview

OctoAcme follows an iterative, customer-first delivery model. Every project is owned by a named Project Manager (PM) and Product Manager (PdM), and progresses through a structured lifecycle from initiation through retrospective. We emphasize clear ownership, data-informed decisions, psychological safety, and small, testable increments at every stage.

## Project Lifecycle

| Stage | Description |
|---|---|
| **Initiation** | Validate the business need, align stakeholders, and decide whether to proceed to planning. |
| **Planning** | Define scope, resources, milestones, dependencies, and the sprint backlog. |
| **Execution & Tracking** | Build, test, review, and iterate; track progress against milestones and the Definition of Done. |
| **Risk & Communication** | Maintain the risk register and ensure the right information reaches the right people at the right time. |
| **Release & Deployment** | Deploy, verify, and announce the change; confirm acceptance criteria are met in production. |
| **Retrospective & Continuous Improvement** | Capture learnings, celebrate wins, and turn action items into measurable process improvements. |
